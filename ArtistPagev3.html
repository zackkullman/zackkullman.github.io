About the Band

Shinedown is a hard rock band from Jacksonville, Florida which was founded in 2001. The members of the band Shinedown are Brent Smith (Singer), Eric Bass (Bass), Zach Myers (Guitarist), and Barry Kerch (Drums). They've released six albums to this day and are planning on releasing their seventh on April 22nd, 2022.
