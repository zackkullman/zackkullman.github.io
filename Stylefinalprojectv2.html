ABOUT OHIO HOCKEY

Growth of the Sport

Hockey has never really been considered a top sport in the state of Ohio; however ever since the arrival of the Columbus Blue Jackets the sport of hockey has grown exponentially. To this day Ohio is home to 14,000 registered players and over 50 ice rinks, 80 youth hockey organizations and 80 high school varsity programs.
